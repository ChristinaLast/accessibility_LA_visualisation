Finding the Fault Lines: Accessibility to jobs in Los Angeles County
Introduction
This visualisation showcases statistics describing access to employment opportunities
in Los Angeles County, The data is collected from the census bureau, and data provided by
GeoTrans Lab in UC Santa Barbara Geography Dept. The visualisation shows
  • The level of access to jobs in each Neighbourhood
  • A summary of the age segment and ethnicity of each neighbourhood
  • The level of accessibility and percentage of household cost spent on transportation
